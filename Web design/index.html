Math competitions

Standard school math curriculum is really boring. You learn a formula, and apply it to problems
over and over again. However, math competitions are different. These competitions encourage different
types of thinking. Instead of using only formulas, these problems require creative solutions. If you can't get
many problems correct, don't be discouraged! These problems are meant to be hard.
