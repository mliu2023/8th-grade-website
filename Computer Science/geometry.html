Geometry

One of the main focuses of geometry is triangles. There are a few lines and points drawn inside triangles that have special properties.

Circumcenter

The circumcenter is the intersection of the perpendicular bisectors of the sides. The circumcenter is equidistant from all three verticies.

Incenter

The incenter is the intersection of the angle bisectors of the three angles. The incenter is equdistant from all three sides.

Orthocenter

The orthocenter is the intersection of the alititudes of the triangle.

Centroid

The centroid is the intersection of the medians of the triangle. Each median is split in a 2:1 ratio.
